Historical Weather News

2004 Indian Ocean Tsunami

Date: December 26, 2004

One of the most devastating natural disasters in modern history, the Indian Ocean Tsunami severely impacted Sri Lanka. Over 30,000 lives were lost, and entire coastal communities were destroyed by the giant waves.

2016 Floods and Landslides

Date: May 15-23, 2016

Severe monsoonal rains led to deadly floods and landslides in Sri Lanka. Nearly 200 people died, and many were left homeless as the floodwaters devastated cities and villages alike.

1978 Cyclone

Date: December 23-24, 1978

A powerful cyclone struck the northern and eastern coasts, causing massive damage to homes and infrastructure. Winds reaching up to 120 km/h claimed over 1,000 lives and disrupted the fishing and agriculture sectors.

2017 Drought

Date: January - June 2017

One of the worst droughts in recent history, the 2017 drought affected over 2 million people, leading to severe water shortages and an agricultural crisis.

2020 Cyclone Burevi

Date: December 2-3, 2020

Cyclone Burevi brought heavy rains and strong winds to Sri Lanka, causing localized flooding and power outages, especially in the northern and eastern provinces.
